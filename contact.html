Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance for regular appointments and 3-4 weeks for custom designs or special events.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, we accept walk-ins based on availability. However, appointments are recommended to ensure we can accommodate you at your preferred time.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and our studio gift cards.

Can I bring my own nail polish?

Yes, you're welcome to bring your own polish. However, we cannot guarantee the quality or longevity of external products.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer special rates for groups of 4 or more. Please contact us for details on group packages and event bookings.

What should I do if I need to cancel?

Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. You can call us or use the link in your confirmation email to reschedule.
