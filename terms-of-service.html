Services Description

Luxe Nail Studio provides professional nail care services including:

  • Classic and gel manicures
  • Nail art and custom designs
  • Gel and acrylic extensions
  • Pedicure services
  • Nail treatments and repairs
  • Consultation services

All services are performed by licensed nail technicians following industry standards and health regulations.

Booking and Appointments

Scheduling

  • Appointments can be booked online, by phone, or in-person
  • Advance booking is recommended, especially for custom designs
  • Walk-in appointments are subject to availability
  • Booking requires valid contact information

Cancellation Policy

  • 24-hour notice required for cancellations
  • Late cancellations may incur a fee of 50% of the service cost
  • No-shows will be charged the full service amount
  • Repeated no-shows may result in loss of booking privileges

Late Arrival

  • Arrivals more than 15 minutes late may result in shortened service time
  • We will do our best to accommodate, but cannot guarantee full service time
  • Full price will be charged for the originally booked service

Payment Terms

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Cash (exact change appreciated)
  • All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit cards
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Studio gift cards

Pricing

  • Prices are as listed on our current price menu
  • Custom designs and complex nail art may have additional charges
  • Prices are subject to change without notice
  • Deposits may be required for extensive services or group bookings

Refunds

  • Refunds are provided at the discretion of management
  • Service issues must be reported within 24 hours
  • Gift cards are non-refundable
  • Deposits are non-refundable for cancellations within 24 hours

Health and Safety

Client Responsibility

  • Inform your technician of any allergies, medical conditions, or medications
  • Report any skin irritation or nail issues immediately
  • Do not use services if you have fungal infections or open wounds
  • Follow aftercare instructions provided by your technician

Our Commitment

  • All tools are properly sterilized between clients
  • Single-use items are disposed of properly
  • Studio maintains high cleanliness standards
  • Technicians follow proper sanitation protocols
